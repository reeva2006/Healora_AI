Core Capabilities

Everything You Need,
Even Off the Grid

Healora AI packs a full clinical decision-support system into a low-resource device — no connectivity needed for core functions.

🧠
On-Device TFLite ML
TensorFlow Lite models run entirely on-device. Symptom classification and risk scoring happen locally — no cloud round-trip, no data leaks.
🆘
Emergency SOS
One-tap SOS triggers GPS location capture and dispatches an SMS to emergency contacts and the nearest clinic — even without internet via SMS fallback.
☁️
Firebase Cloud Sync
All records are queued offline and synced automatically when connectivity returns. Firebase Firestore ensures zero data loss and seamless handoff.
📹
WebRTC Doctor Consult
Real-time video consultations with remote doctors via WebRTC peer-to-peer. Adaptive bitrate ensures usability on 2G/3G links.
💬
Natural Language Input
Patients describe symptoms in plain language or local dialect. NLP extracts clinical entities and feeds them into the triage engine.
📍
GPS + SMS Fallback
When data networks fail, Healora encodes location and triage data into an SMS so first responders always know where to go and what to expect.

Data Privacy

Your Data Never Leaves
Your Device — Unless You Say So

Privacy is non-negotiable. Healora AI is designed with a zero-trust, offline-first privacy model that puts patients in full control.

🔒
On-Device Inference
ML models run locally. Raw symptom data never leaves the phone.
🔐
AES-256 Encryption
All stored health records are encrypted at rest on-device.
🧬
Federated Learning
Model improvements are derived from aggregated, anonymized gradients — never raw patient data.
⚖️
HIPAA / GDPR Ready
Architecture is compliant with major health-data regulations globally.
